Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

FolioSense ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website foliosenseapp.com and the portfolio analysis application available at foliosenseapp.com/dashboard. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using FolioSense, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Your portfolio data

When you upload a CSV or Excel file containing your portfolio holdings, that data is sent to our backend server solely to perform the analysis you requested. We do not store your portfolio data. It is processed in memory and discarded when your session ends.

We do not:

  • Save or log your portfolio holdings to any database
  • Associate your portfolio data with any identifier
  • Share your portfolio data with any third party
  • Use your portfolio data for advertising or profiling

2. Information we collect automatically

Like most websites, we collect certain information automatically when you visit FolioSense:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, and referring URL. This data is used for security, debugging, and aggregate analytics only.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes from our own systems.

3. Google AdSense and advertising

FolioSense displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to FolioSense and other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

We have no control over the cookies placed by Google AdSense. For more information, see Google's advertising policy.

4. Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar) to understand how visitors use FolioSense in aggregate — which pages are most visited, how long people stay, and where they come from. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you individually.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. No account, no personal data

FolioSense does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information as part of the core service. If you choose to contact us by email, we will only use that information to respond to your inquiry.

6. Data security

All data transmitted between your browser and FolioSense is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Our backend servers are hosted on Railway, a SOC 2 compliant platform. While we take reasonable security precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's privacy

FolioSense is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Links to third-party sites

FolioSense may contain links to external websites. We have no control over the content or privacy practices of those sites and are not responsible for them. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FolioSense after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (such as GDPR for EU residents or CCPA for California residents), including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Since we do not store portfolio data or personal information from core usage, there is typically no data to access or delete. If you have questions, please contact us.
