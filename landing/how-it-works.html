How it works

From spreadsheet to strategy in 30 seconds.

FolioSense is designed to be the fastest way to understand what your investments are actually doing — and what to do about it.

1
Export your holdings
Exporta tus posiciones

Get your file from any broker.

Most brokers let you export your holdings as a CSV or Excel file. FolioSense accepts both. Here's how to export from the most common platforms:

  • Robinhood — Account → Statements & History → Export
  • Fidelity — Accounts → Portfolio → Download
  • Charles Schwab — Accounts → Positions → Export to spreadsheet
  • Interactive Brokers — Reports → Activity → Positions

Your file needs at least three columns: the stock symbol (ticker), the number of shares, and the price you paid. Sector and ROI columns are optional but give you richer analysis.

Not sure about the format? Try our sample file — it loads with example data so you can explore all the features before uploading your own.

Obtén tu archivo desde cualquier broker.

La mayoría de los brokers te permiten exportar tus posiciones como archivo CSV o Excel. FolioSense acepta ambos. Así se hace en las plataformas más comunes:

  • Robinhood — Cuenta → Historial → Exportar
  • Fidelity — Cuentas → Portafolio → Descargar
  • Charles Schwab — Cuentas → Posiciones → Exportar a hoja de cálculo
  • Interactive Brokers — Reportes → Actividad → Posiciones

Tu archivo necesita al menos tres columnas: el símbolo (ticker), la cantidad de acciones y el precio que pagaste. Las columnas de sector y ROI son opcionales pero enriquecen el análisis.

2
Upload and analyze
Sube y analiza

Your portfolio analyzed instantly.

Drag and drop your file onto the FolioSense app. That's it. No forms, no email, no password.

Within seconds you'll see your complete portfolio breakdown: total value, a risk score from 0 to 100, how concentrated your holdings are, and which sectors you're exposed to.

Every chart is interactive. Hover over any data point to see the details. The scatter plot shows you every stock as a point — risk on the X axis, return on the Y axis — so you can instantly spot which positions are high risk and low reward.

Tu portafolio analizado en segundos.

Arrastra y suelta tu archivo en la app de FolioSense. Eso es todo. Sin formularios, sin correo, sin contraseña.

En segundos verás el desglose completo de tu portafolio: valor total, puntaje de riesgo del 0 al 100, qué tan concentradas están tus posiciones y a qué sectores estás expuesto.

Cada gráfico es interactivo. Pasa el cursor sobre cualquier punto para ver los detalles. El scatter plot muestra cada acción como un punto — riesgo en el eje X, retorno en el eje Y — para que identifiques al instante las posiciones de alto riesgo y bajo rendimiento.

Chat with your AI advisor
Chatea con tu asesor IA

Ask anything. Get real answers.

Once your portfolio is loaded, an AI advisor is ready to answer any question about it — in plain language, using your actual numbers.

You can ask things like:

  • "Am I too heavily invested in tech?"
  • "Which of my stocks has the worst risk-to-return ratio?"
  • "What would happen to my portfolio if the tech sector dropped 20%?"
  • "Should I be worried about my NVDA position?"

The AI knows your exact holdings, weights, sectors, and ROI. It gives advice specific to your portfolio, not generic tips from a finance blog.

FolioSense is an educational tool. Nothing here is professional financial advice.
